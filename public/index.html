The Future of Micropayments


Join us on November 5th for a virtual unconference exploring the future of money at human scale.


5 November 2020 9am-4pm PST
Online through Nexus Events



Manifesto


Money has become a form of communication. It can separate truth from fiction, it can signal value - as well as support content and creators that we love.


We’re bringing together content creators, artists, programmers, designers, cryptographers and thinkers to inspire each other, share points of view and discuss the design and architecture of exchanging value.


The conference will be held through Nexus Events, a flexible virtual platform that will allow us to provide presentations, breakout sessions and networking sessions to bring complementary skills and interests.


If you’ve ever had frustrations with online payments, the creative economy, or simply want to explore the future of payments at human scale, please join us!
