THE FUTURE OF MICROPAYMENTS

OVERVIEW

Join us online on 5th of November at Nexus Events at 9am PST at the Future of Micro Payments Conference.

This is a new conference on the missing middle of payments online. Currencies, exchanges and empowering creators is our mission. Micro currency is a hot idea with many different opportunities. This conference is a way of helping people meet each other, inspiring each other an setting the stage for a new sector of the fintech industry. We bring together creators, makers, developers and inventors to hear each other and connect on solving one of the biggest small problems of our time.

We're bringing together minds from the fintech industry, artists, programmers, designers, cryptographers, creators and consumers to inspire each other, share points of view, discuss new ideas, and look for solutions together.

We are organizing presentation, lightning talks, speed networking and birds of a feather using the Nexus Events online conference tools. We have a fun fast speed networking session run by Nexus Events to introduce people to each other based on complementary interests.

THE CHALLENGE

Money is a form of communication as well as a medium of payment. Being able to make small direct payments for a product or service also signals more widely which products or services are best.

However today there is a transaction gap at the lower end of the payments spectrum, especially online, due to frictions around payments. International payments are hindered, and the unbanked disproportionately cannot participate in emerging future economies.

Empowering people to directly pay each other online, as an alternative to advertising or subscriptions may at the very least help creators and will help catalyze rich content and experiences for consumers. At the very most we may create opportunities for people around the world and help foster an explosion of creativity and communication.
