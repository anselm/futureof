THE FUTURE OF MICROPAYMENTS EVENT

About

A new conference on the missing middle of payments online. Currencies, exchanges and empowering creator. Micro currency is a hot idea with many different opportunities. This conference is a way of helping people meet each other, inspiring each other an setting the stage for a new sector of the fintech industry. We bring together creators, makers, developers and inventors to hear each other and connect on solving one of the biggest small problems of our time.
